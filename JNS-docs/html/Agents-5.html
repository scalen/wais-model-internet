5. Examples

Believe it or not, there is nothing more to say about agents in JNS. The rest is just technical details. The best thing to do is to look at

  • jns/agent/Agent.java
  • jns/agent/CO_Agent.java
  • jns/agent/CL_Agent.java

Those files contain the interfaces agents use and each function in them is well documented. You might also want to look at SimpleGoBackN.java and RandomSource and RandomSink.java for some examples.
